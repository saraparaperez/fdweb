Origin & Concept

ÄRA is a contemporary graphic brand that explores the visual language of the present era through limited editorial editions.

The name comes from the German word Ära. It reflects a critical observation of the current time, its rhythms, visual saturation, and constant transformation.

ÄRA does not aim to define the era, but to translate it into visual systems and editorial objects.
