New Art, New Life

Illustrated calendar inspired by the surrealist universe of René Magritte, reinterpreted through a contemporary lens. Each month features an original illustration that draws on the artist’s iconic elements—such as the hidden, the absurd, or the transformation of the everyday—and reimagines them in minimalist scenes full of symbolism. The compositions play with visual ambiguity, emptiness, and the breaking of logic, striking a balance between the conceptual and the aesthetic. The calendar’s design is clean and understated, with a subtle typographic structure that gives full prominence to the images. An invitation to view the passage of time from a new, poetic, and thought-provoking perspective.
