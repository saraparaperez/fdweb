Illustrated campaign concept for Four Seasons and Veuve Clicquot

This project is an illustrated campaign concept for the luxury hotel group Four Seasons in collaboration with Veuve Clicquot, designed around three iconic destinations: Thailand, Marrakech, and the French Riviera. Each illustration captures the unique spirit of its location, combining local cultural elements, architectural motifs, and natural landscapes with the elegance and festive energy of Veuve Clicquot. The visual narrative celebrates the art of travel and refined hospitality through a seasonal lens — golden sunsets in Thailand, vibrant markets and desert hues in Marrakech, and breezy coastal glamour in the Riviera. The compositions are rich in detail and color, exuding warmth, exclusivity, and celebration. Designed for both print and digital formats, the campaign creates a cohesive visual identity that reflects the essence of both brands while inviting viewers to immerse themselves in a luxurious, illustrated journey.
