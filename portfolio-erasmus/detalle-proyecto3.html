Gulliver's Travels

This project is a special edition of the classic book Gulliver’s Travels, created to celebrate its 300th anniversary. The design focuses on typography as the central element, using typographic compositions to reinterpret the story in a modern and innovative way. Through carefully crafted layouts and typographic art, this edition offers a fresh perspective that bridges the classic narrative with contemporary design trends. The project highlights how typography can become a powerful visual tool to convey meaning and emotion, making this version both a tribute to the original and a unique artistic expression.
