Matria

This project is a UI/UX design for a vegan and vegetarian restaurant app, created in Figma. The concept emphasizes clean, user-friendly navigation with a visually appealing interface that reflects the freshness and natural essence of plant-based cuisine. The design features earthy tones, organic shapes, and high-quality food imagery to enhance user engagement. Core functionalities include browsing the menu, making reservations, and ordering for pickup or delivery. The app aligns with the values of health, sustainability, and mindful eating.
