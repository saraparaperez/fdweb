PhDay Logo Design

This logo was created for the 8th annual PhDay of the Complutense University of Madrid, a key academic event that brings together students, researchers, and professionals in pharmaceutical sciences. The design is rooted in the iconic symbol of pharmacy: the chalice and the snake, representing knowledge, healing, and the discipline’s classical heritage. By integrating these elements in a clean, contemporary composition, the logo pays tribute to tradition while aligning with the innovative and scientific spirit of the event. The visual identity aims to reinforce the academic prestige of the Faculty of Pharmacy, while creating a memorable and distinctive image for this special edition.
