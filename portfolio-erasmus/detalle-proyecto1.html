Between Light and Skin

Photographic studio project that explores female sensuality and elegance through a carefully designed chromatic and lighting setup. Using warm-toned backgrounds, it creates a vibrant visual environment that enhances the contrast with the model’s black clothing, styled with a balance between sexy and sophisticated. Each image aims to convey strength, presence, and confidence, using light as a narrative tool to sculpt the body and emphasize attitude. This series becomes a visual play between intensity and restraint, where color not only frames the scene but also evokes emotion.
