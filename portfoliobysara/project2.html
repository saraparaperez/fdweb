2025

branding, packaging

about:

“Al Grano” is a coffee brand designed for people who value clarity, honesty, and straightforward experiences.

The concept is built around a double meaning: the coffee bean itself and the idea of getting straight to the point. This duality defines the brand’s personality and guides every design decision.

The visual identity focuses on clean compositions, bold typography, and concise messages. Minimal graphic elements and direct language reflect the brand’s philosophy of avoiding unnecessary distractions.
