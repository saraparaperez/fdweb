( al grano )

2025

branding, packaging

about:

“Al Grano” is a coffee brand designed for people who value clarity, honesty, and straightforward experiences.
The concept is built around a double meaning: the coffee bean itself and the idea of getting straight to the point. This duality defines the brand’s personality and guides every design decision.
The visual identity focuses on clean compositions, bold typography, and concise messages. Minimal graphic elements and direct language reflect the brand’s philosophy of avoiding unnecessary distractions, letting the product and the message speak for themselves. The use of short phrases and strong contrasts reinforces the idea of simplicity and intention.
Through its branding and packaging, Al Grano invites customers to slow down without going in circles, to enjoy quality coffee, and to embrace a clear, uncomplicated approach—both in design and in everyday life.
