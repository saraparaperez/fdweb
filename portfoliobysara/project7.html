An illustrated campaign concept for Four Seasons in collaboration with Veuve Clicquot, inspired by three iconic destinations: Thailand, Marrakech, and the French Riviera. Each illustration captures the essence of its location through cultural references, architecture, and landscape, combined with the elegance and celebratory spirit of Veuve Clicquot.

Designed for print and digital formats, the campaign creates a cohesive visual system that celebrates travel, luxury, and refined hospitality.
