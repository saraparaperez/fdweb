about:

A logo creation for the 8th annual PhDay at the Complutense University of Madrid, an academic event gathering students, researchers, and professionals in pharmaceutical sciences.

The design is based on the traditional symbol of pharmacy—the chalice and the snake representing knowledge, healing, and the discipline’s heritage. These elements are combined into a clean, contemporary composition, bridging classical tradition with the innovative and scientific spirit of the event.

The visual identity strengthens the Faculty of Pharmacy’s academic prestige while creating a distinctive and memorable image for this special edition of PhDay.
