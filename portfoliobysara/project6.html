about:

A label design project for a series of three beverage cans inspired by Madrid. Each label explores a different aspect of the city while maintaining a cohesive visual system across the set.

The first can highlights iconic elements and landmarks of Madrid, capturing its energy and character through playful illustration. The second draws inspiration from the city’s metro, using geometric patterns and symbolic references to urban transit. The third celebrates the tradition of San Isidro with a floral motif of red carnations, combining cultural symbolism with vibrant, expressive color.
