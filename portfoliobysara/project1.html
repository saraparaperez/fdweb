( escape )

2025

branding, packaging

about:

“Escape” is a wine label designed for a youthful audience seeking moments of relaxation and spontaneity.

The concept is built around the idea of escaping from everyday routine, turning each bottle into a small visual and sensory getaway.

The visual identity features bright, eye-catching colors combined with playful, lighthearted illustrations that evoke freedom and movement.

Through its label design, Escape encourages consumers to break from monotony, enjoy the present moment, and rediscover wine as an accessible, expressive product with a strong personality.
