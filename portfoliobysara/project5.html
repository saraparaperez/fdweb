A brand redesign of ING’s visual identity, aimed at modernizing the brand and reinforcing its position as a digital, accessible, and innovative bank..
The design introduces a cleaner, more contemporary logo alongside a refreshed color palette, custom typography, and a flexible graphic system that balances technological precision with a sense of human warmth.
The brand manual provides comprehensive guidelines covering logo usage, color systems, typography, photography, and layouts, ensuring a consistent and cohesive visual language across all applications.
