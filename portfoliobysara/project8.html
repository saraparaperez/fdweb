about:

This project is a UI/UX design for a vegan and vegetarian restaurant app, developed as a digital experience focused on clarity, usability, and visual balance. The concept is based on creating an intuitive interface that reflects the natural, fresh character of plant-based cuisine through thoughtful layout and interaction design.

The app includes essential features such as menu exploration, reservations, and ordering options, all integrated seamlessly into the interface. Overall, the design aligns with values of health, sustainability, and mindful consumption, translating these principles into a coherent and user-centered digital product.
