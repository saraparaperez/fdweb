about:

A cover and back cover design for the doctoral thesis “Search for antivirals for the treatment of coronavirus-related diseases.” The project aims to visually communicate the scientific content in a clear and accessible way while paying tribute to the author’s Andalusian heritage.

The design combines molecular and research-inspired graphics with geometric patterns reminiscent of traditional Andalusian tiles, creating a balance between academic formality and visual appeal. Composition, typography, and color palette were carefully developed to merge scientific precision with cultural reference, resulting in a cover that conveys both innovation and tradition.
